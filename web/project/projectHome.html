Instructions

1. Do you have a group invite code?
No: Click the "Start a Group" button to create a group. Then send the invite code to others in your group and click "Continue".
Yes: Click the "Join a Group" button. Then paste the group invite code into the field and click "Join Group".

2. Fill out the form
Enter your top three restaurant choices along with your least favorite choice. Then click "Submit".

3. View the results
Using the table of information on the results page to make your final decision.
